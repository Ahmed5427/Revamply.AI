1 Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you use our services, we collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number (optional), and country code
  • Business Information: Business description, department, current processes, pain points, business goals, and process frequency
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our live chat support, emails, or contact forms

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and navigation paths
  • Technical Data: Submission IDs, timestamps, and session information

To Provide Our Services

To Communicate With You

Send your AI blueprints via email, respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and schedule consultations

To Improve Our Services

Analyze usage patterns, understand user needs, enhance our AI algorithms, and develop new features

To Manage Our Business

For Security and Compliance
