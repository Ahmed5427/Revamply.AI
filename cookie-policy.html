1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

What Cookies Do

  • Remember your preferences
  • Keep you logged in
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze how you use the site

Cookie Safety

  • Cannot carry viruses
  • Cannot install malware
  • Cannot access your files
  • Only store specific website data

2 Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes to enhance your experience on our website:

Essential Website Functionality

Enable core features like form submissions, session management, and security

Performance and Analytics

Understand how visitors interact with our site to improve user experience

Personalization

Remember your preferences and settings for future visits

Communication

Enable features like live chat support and customer service tools

Security

Protect against fraud and ensure secure access to our services

OPTIONAL

6 Cookie Duration

Cookies can be temporary or persistent:

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser.

Used for:

  • • Maintaining your session
  • • Form submissions
  • • Shopping cart functionality

Persistent Cookies

Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

Used for:

  • • Remembering preferences
  • • Analytics and performance
  • • User recognition

8 Other Tracking Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies:

Web Beacons

Small graphic images (also known as "pixel tags" or "clear GIFs") that may be included on our website and emails to track page views and email opens.

Local Storage

Browser storage mechanisms (localStorage, sessionStorage) that allow websites to store data locally on your device for improved performance.

Device Fingerprinting

Collection of device and browser information to create a unique identifier for analytics purposes (we use this minimally and only for security).
